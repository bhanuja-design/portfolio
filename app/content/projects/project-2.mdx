# Project Two

Redesigning onboarding to help new users reach their first meaningful success faster.

## Problem

New sign-ups were dropping off before completing setup. Analytics showed that most users never finished the initial configuration steps required to use the product.

## Approach

I audited the existing onboarding funnel, ran contextual interviews with recent sign-ups, and designed a progressive disclosure flow that broke setup into smaller, guided steps with clear progress indicators.

## Outcome

Activation rates increased within the first month after launch. Users reported feeling less overwhelmed, and the simplified flow became the template for future feature introductions.
